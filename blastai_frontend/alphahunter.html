Concept
Bitcoin's Layer 2 (BTC Layer 2) refers to a secondary framework or protocol that is built on top of an existing blockchain system. The main goal of these Layer 2 solutions is to solve the scalability and speed issues that can be encountered with the base layer (Layer 1) of a blockchain like Bitcoin.One of the most well-known examples of a Layer 2 solution on Bitcoin is the Lightning Network.
Latest News
Growth of the Bitcoin Layer-2 Ecosystem

The Bitcoin Layer-2 ecosystem is showing signs of significant growth, particularly with the development of networks like the Lightning Network, Stacks, Liquid, and Rootstock. These Layer-2 solutions are designed to address the limitations of the Bitcoin network, such as scalability and transaction speed. The growth is partly inspired by the success of Layer-2 projects in the Ethereum ecosystem, such as Base from Coinbase, Arbitrum, Optimism, and Polygon.

Bitfinity Raises Funds at a $130 Million Valuation

Bitfinity, a Bitcoin Layer 2 network that's compatible with the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) and based on the Internet Computer protocol, has raised a total of $7 million in funding. This funding round has brought Bitfinity's valuation to $130 million. The network is focused on enhancing decentralized finance activities on the Bitcoin blockchain, and its EVM compatibility allows Ethereum developers to build Bitcoin-enabled decentralized applications on Bitfinity.
